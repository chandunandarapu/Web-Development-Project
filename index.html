  • Which are the top engineering colleges?

    Some of the top engineering colleges offering B.Tech are:
    Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs): Currently, there are 16 IITs in the country:- IIT Bombay , IIT Delhi , IIT Madras , IIT Kanpur , IIT Roorkee , IIT Kharagpur , IIT (BHU) Varanasi , IIT Gandhinagar , IIT Guwahati , IIT Patna , IIT Mandi , IIT Ropar , IIT Bhubaneswar , IIT Hyderabad , IIT Jodhpur and IIT Indore.
    National Institutes of Technology (NITs): There are around 30 NITs in the country. Indian Institutes of Information Technology (IIITs)Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) College of Engineering, Guindy (CEG)Delhi Technological University (DTU)

  • Which is the best course to pursue after B.E /B.Tech?

    After B.E. /B.Tech, you have your options:
    M.E./ M.Tech to study on the technical sideMBA, if you wish to move your career on the management trackGain work experience for a couple of years in private or public sector. That may give you a clearer picture of your interest area to decide between M.Tech and MBA.

  • Which are the top private engineering colleges?

    While selecting the best college, you need to check important parameters such as recognition, year of establishment, batch size, syllabus, faculty, fees, placements, etc. Some of the top private engineering colleges are:
    PSG College of Technology, CoimbatoreThapar Institute of Engineering and TechnologyAmity UniversityLovely Professional University, JalandharR V College of Engineering, BangaloreSSN College of Engineering, ChennaiCoimbatore Institute of TechnologyManipal Institute of Technology, Manipal
